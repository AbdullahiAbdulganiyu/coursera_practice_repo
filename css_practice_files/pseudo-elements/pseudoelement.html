• Based in Chicago, Little Lemon is family-owned Mediterranean resturant, focused on traditional recipes and served with a modern twist.
  • The chefs draw inspiration from Italian, Greek, and turkish culture and have menu of 12-15 items that they rotate seasonally. The resturant has a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a popular place for a meal any time of the day
  • Little Lemon is owned by two Italian brothers, Marion and Adrian, who moved tothe United States to pursue their shared dream of owning a resturant. To craft the menu, Mario relies on family recipes and his experience as a chef in Italy.
